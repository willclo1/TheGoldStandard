About Us

We are The Gold Standard, a Baylor student team dedicated to researching and analyzing real community challenges. This semester, we’re partnering with the Greater Waco Sports Commission to identify key issues and develop innovative solutions that support athletics and community engagement in Waco.

Our Partner

The Greater Waco Sports Commission works to promote, attract, and support sports activities that positively impact the community. We’re proud to collaborate with them in driving sports development across the Waco area.
